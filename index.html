Nested Wikipedia

How to use this tool:

  • Search for your main article of interest by typing it into the search bar above and then clicking the . The top 10 related terms will appear just below the search bar. Click on any of them to view its article in the main window.
  • Click the button to the right of the magnifying glass to toggle the view of the top 10 search results.
  • If there's a word/term you want to know more about in the main window, highlight it and (if there is a Wikipedia entry on it) it will appear under the "Supporting Articles" section to the right. You can also highlight unknown words/terms that you find within supporting articles.
  • You can remove an individual supporting article, just click on the in the top right corner of the supporting article box. To clear out all supporting articles, click on the in the top right corner of your window.
  • Finally, if you decide that you want to move a supporting document into the main window, click on the in the top right corner of the supporting article box.

I was inspired to make this tool when I first started learning about programming. It can be difficult and overwhelming to learn a completely new concept, because you come across so many new terms all at once. Hopefully this tool will help and encourage you to learn about big, new ideas.
